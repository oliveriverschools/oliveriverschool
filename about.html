About the school

A nurturing start with steady primary growth.

Olive River Schools Ladysmith welcomes learners from Grade RR to Grade 5.

Our approach

Children need structure, warmth, and room to grow.

Our school environment supports literacy, numeracy, social development, creativity, character formation, and growing independence in an age-appropriate setting.

01

Mission

To help every learner grow in knowledge, manners, confidence, and responsibility through the primary school years.

02

Vision

To be a trusted primary school for families in Ladysmith and surrounding communities.

03

Values

Respect, care, excellence, faith, discipline, kindness, and service guide the school day.

RR-5

Our complete school focus.

  • Grade RREarly readiness and routine
  • Grade RPre-primary foundations
  • Grade 1Reading, writing, and number confidence
  • Grade 2Growing independence and fluency
  • Grade 3Building confidence across core subjects
  • Grade 4Intermediate learning and responsibility
  • Grade 5Preparation for senior primary growth

Daily life

Clear routines, caring adults, and bright learning moments.

Children learn best when they feel safe, seen, and encouraged. Olive River Schools supports each stage from early readiness through Grade 5, so teaching, care, and facilities can match the needs of growing learners.
